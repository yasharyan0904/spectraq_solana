  How the contracts actually get called

  There are two CPI patterns in this codebase:

  1. User → program (deposit/withdraw): standard Anchor RPC. User signs a tx whose only instruction is program.methods.depositUsdc(...). The program's system_program and token_program calls are CPIs but stay inside the program's
   own jurisdiction.

  slippage floor in Rust before delegating to Jupiter.
